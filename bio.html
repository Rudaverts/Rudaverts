About me

My name is Ruben Everts i'm 28 years old and i was born in Caracas Venzuela. I have been taking pictures since i was 18, and i've been in love with photography ever since. I have had my ups and downs but now i found my ground and i'm working my best to reach my goal into turning photography in my fulltime job. Some days i feel very confident about me and my work, and even when i don't... i still do my best to perfectionate my craft, because the beauty that I see through my lens is meant to be seen by everyone else, so I bid you welcome into my humble gallery.
